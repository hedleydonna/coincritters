# Willow User Guide

Welcome to Willow! This guide will help you get started with the application.

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the home page
2. Click **"Sign up here!"**
3. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Display Name** (optional): Choose a name that will be displayed on your dashboard
   - **Email**: Your email address (required)
   - **Password**: Choose a secure password (minimum 6 characters)
   - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password
4. Click **"Sign up"**

### Logging In

1. Visit the home page
2. Click **"Already have an account? Log in"**
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click **"Log in"**

### Forgot Your Password?

If you've forgotten your password:

1. On the login page, click **"Forgot your password?"**
2. Enter your email address
3. Click **"Send me reset password instructions"**
4. Check your email for a password reset link
5. Click the link in the email to set a new password

## Your Dashboard

Once logged in, you'll see your personalized dashboard with:

- **Welcome Message**: Greeting using your display name or email
- **Baby Willow**: Your willow tree is ready to grow!
- **Edit Profile**: Update your display name and email
- **Sign Out**: Log out of your account

## Managing Your Profile

### Editing Your Profile

1. Click **"Edit Profile"** on your dashboard
2. Update your **Display Name** or **Email**
3. Click **"Update Profile"**

**Note**: You can update your display name and email without entering your password.

### Changing Your Password

To change your password:

1. Click **"Forgot your password?"** on the login page
2. Follow the password reset instructions sent to your email

## Account Management

### Canceling Your Account

If you wish to delete your account:

1. Go to **Edit Profile**
2. Scroll to the **"Danger Zone"** section
3. Click **"Cancel my account"**
4. Confirm the deletion

**Warning**: This action cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted.

## Features

### Display Name

- Your display name is optional
- If you don't set one, your email username will be used
- You can change it anytime from your profile settings

### Email

- Your email is used for login and password resets
- It must be unique (no two accounts can share the same email)
- You can update it from your profile settings

## Security

- Passwords are encrypted and stored securely
- Password reset links expire after 6 hours
- Each password reset link can only be used once

## Getting Help

If you encounter any issues:

1. Check that you're using the correct email and password
2. Use the "Forgot Password" feature if you can't remember your password
3. Ensure your email address is correct for password resets

## Tips

- Choose a memorable display name to personalize your experience
- Keep your email address up to date for password resets
- Use a strong, unique password for your account
